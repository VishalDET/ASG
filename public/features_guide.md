# ASG Platform - User & Features Guide

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to use the ASG (Antigravity Scratch & Gift) platform, covering both the Customer experience and Administrative controls.

---

## üì± Customer Section

The Customer section is a mobile-responsive web portal where users can play and win.

### 1. Access & Registration
- **Step 1**: Open the portal and enter your **Mobile Number**.
- **Step 2**: If you are a new user, you will be prompted to fill in your name, email, and preferences (Food/Alcohol).
- **Step 3**: Click **Register** to enter the dashboard.

### 2. The Scratch Card Game
- **Feature**: Every day, you get one fresh scratch card.
- **How to play**:
    - Tap on the "Try Your Luck" button on your dashboard.
    - Use your finger (mobile) or mouse (desktop) to "scratch" the silver layer on the card.
    - Once 70% of the card is cleared, your gift will be automatically revealed.
- **Winning**: Each offer comes with a unique **Code** (e.g., `OFFER-123`) and an **Expiry Date**.

### 3. Your Profile & Offer History
- **Access**: Click the user icon in the top right.
- **View History**: See all the offers you've revealed in the past.
- **Status Tags**: 
    - `Available`: Ready to be used.
    - `Redeemed`: Already used at an outlet.
- **Copy Code**: Tap the "Copy" icon next to any code to show it to the restaurant staff.

## üõ†Ô∏è Admin Section

The Admin section is a management suite for tracking performance and processing customer wins.

### 1. Dashboard (Analytics)
- **Top Metrics**: View Total Customers, Active Offers, and Total Redemptions at a glance.
- **Date Filters**: Switch between 7, 30, or 90 days to see engagement trends.
- **Charts**: 
    - **Engagement**: Shows how many users are active daily.
    - **Redemptions**: Compares "Revealed" vs "Redeemed" cards.

### 2. Offer Manager
Create and control the "Gifts" that customers can win.
- **Add New Offer**:
    - Enter Title (e.g., "10% Off Main Course").
    - Set **Weight**: This determines the probability (Chance) of this offer appearing.
    - **Targeting**: Choose if it's for `New`, `Frequent`, or `All` customers.
    - **Expiry**: Set how many days the coupon remains valid after reveal.
- **Manage**: Toggle the switch to make an offer `Active` or `Inactive`.

### 3. Redemption Terminal
**This is the most critical tool for staff at the location.**
- **Process**:
    1. Ask the customer for their unique code.
    2. Enter the code in the terminal and click **Verify**.
    3. View the Customer Name and Offer details to ensure it matches.
    4. Click **Redeem Offer**.
    5. A confirmation box appears‚Äîclick **YES** to finalize. The offer is now marked as "Redeemed" and cannot be used again.

### 4. Customer Database
- **Search**: Find any customer by their name or phone number.
- **History**: View a specific customer's profile to check their total visits and which offers they have claimed.

### 5. Staff Management
- Administrators can create other "Admin" or "Staff" accounts to provide access to the Redemption Terminal.

## üí° Best Practices
- **Daily Engagement**: Encourage customers to visit the portal every day to scratch their card.
- **Validation**: Always use the "Redemption Terminal" before giving the discount to ensure the code hasn't been used before.
- **Analytics**: Check the "Conversion Funnel" in Analytics to see which offers are most popular.
